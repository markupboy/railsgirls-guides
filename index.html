Welcome to Rails Girls Salt Lake!

The amazing Rails Girls Community has developed a set of guides which we'll be working through this weekend. We have forked the guides and made a few small tweaks for simplicity. The originals guides can be found at guides.railsgirls.com. The curriculum can be largely self guided. The best way to learn is to get in there make changes and break stuff. Your coach can provide background, help give you a better depth of understanding, and work with you through any problems you may encounter.

We sincerely hope you enjoy your time at the conference.
